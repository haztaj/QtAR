## Full description (max 4000)
```
Quran Recite listens as you recite and follows along on the page in real time — highlighting exactly which ayah you are reading, the moment you read it. No typing and no searching: open the mushaf, start reciting, and let the page keep up with you.

Built for reciters at every level — from huffaz to beginners and non-native speakers — Quran Recite is designed to track your recitation even as your pace, pronunciation, and melody vary.

Best of all, everything happens ON YOUR DEVICE. Your voice is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. Recognition runs entirely offline after a one-time setup download.

WHAT IT DOES
• Identifies the verse you are reciting from your microphone, live
• Highlights the current ayah on a beautiful KFGQPC mushaf page and turns pages as you go
• Reveals the "up next" ayah so you can flow smoothly from one to the next
• Follows natural pauses and continuous recitation alike
• Riwayah: Hafs 'an 'Asim

WHAT IT IS NOT
• It is not a transcriber and not a tajwid grader. It identifies and tracks the verse, so you can keep your focus on your recitation.

PRIVACY FIRST
• Audio is processed on-device and immediately discarded
• No account, no ads, no tracking, no analytics
• Works fully offline once set up

CURRENT COVERAGE (BETA)
This early release covers Juz 'Amma (Surah 78–114) plus Surah al-Fatihah, al-Baqarah, and Al 'Imran (Surah 1–3). Coverage of the rest of the Qur'an is on the way.

We would love your feedback as we widen coverage and sharpen accuracy. Jazakum Allahu khayran.
```
